The Icelandic Go Association

The Icelandic Go Association is the official association of Go players in Iceland. It consists of a growing number of enthusiastic players working to introducing the game to fellow .

Through the years the association has held a number of tournaments and exhibitions. It runs the Reykjavík Go Club where members meet for discussions and play.
